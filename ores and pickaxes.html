As you can see each pickaxe has a tier that goes along with the description. These tiers are 
Easy, Medium, And Hard. In the ores chart further down you will see each ore has a corresponding tier
these tiers are what the pickaxes can mine. Such as the Jade Pickaxe, it can mine anything Medium tier or below
so that includes all ores with the Medium and Easy tier. Tiers will be further explained in the ores section.
