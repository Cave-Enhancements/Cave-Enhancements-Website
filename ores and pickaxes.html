As you can see each pickaxe has a tier that goes along with the description. These tiers are 
Easy, Medium, And Hard. In the ores chart further down you will see each ore has a corresponding tier
these tiers are what the pickaxes can mine. Such as the Jade Pickaxe, it can mine anything Medium tier or below
so that includes all ores with the Medium and Easy tier. Tiers will be further explained in the ores section.




"Vanilla" Pickaxes!



Vanilla pickaxes work differently in Cave Enhancements. These vanilla pickaxes allow you
to mine the custom ores without having any issues. These use the exact same recipes as the normal pickaxes would just 
make sure when your crafting then that they have "Warheads Cave Enhancements" after the name. If your crafting them 
using the recipe book then, make sure you egt the one with the correct name and not the default one. Keep in mind
that thse pickaxes won't use durability due to a glitch with Mojang. These pickaxes also that there own tier and mining
capabilities.

Ores!



In Cave Enhancements, currently there are around 50 ores this includes 30 ores in the Overworld,
10 ores in the Nether, and 10 in the End. Each of these ores have different uses such as weapons, armor, tools, machines,
and food. In the chart below explains the name of ore, tier, location, and rarity also in this chart includes images of 
what the ores look like. Tiers are catigorized into Easy, Medium, and Hard in the pickaxes section above you can learn what
pickaxes can mine what tiers. Each custom ore cannot be mined by fist and must only be mined by pickaxe. Also, rarity is based in 
four levels Common, Uncommon, Rare, and Very Rare. Each rarity has certain Y level corresponding with it. Common ores can be found 
anywhere from Y level 35-55. Uncommon ores can be found anywhere from Y level 25-41. Rare and Very Rare ores can be found at 
Y levels 5-30.
