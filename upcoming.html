The Builders Update

As you can see from the screenshot above this update will include a slew of new building blocks for you builders! This will finally give Marble, Andesite, Diorite, Limestone, And Granite a purpose for all of your building needs. Also, keep in mind the image above is always changing and what you see is not the final version. This update will be released along side V1.5.1 which will give more ores and stone types more purposes. The blocks shown above are Marble Pillars, Marble Bricks, Smooth Diorite Bricks, Smooth Andesite Bricks, Smooth Granite Bricks, Smooth Marble Bricks, Smooth Marble Flooring, and Marble Slabs. A Beta is planned for this release, so be on the lookout for that!
